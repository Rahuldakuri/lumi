Product Concept
What you're building — in plain language
BoardSense
A slim camera sensor bar that mounts on any existing blackboard or whiteboard. It captures chalk writing in real time, uses AI to organize the notes, and pushes professor-approved content directly into Canvas — with zero change to how professors already teach.
"We don't replace the blackboard. We give it a memory."

YOUR COMPETITIVE MOAT — Why This Is Hard To Copy
Canvas LTI integration takes time and certification — once you're in, you're sticky inside university systems.
Hardware + software together creates a bundled solution that's hard to unbundle and replicate cheaply.
Data network effect: the more boards you train your AI on, the better chalk recognition becomes — a moat that grows with customers.
Your Amazon supply chain background means you can build and ship hardware cost-effectively — something most software founders can't do.
University contracts are long — once a department standardizes on BoardSense, switching costs are high.
